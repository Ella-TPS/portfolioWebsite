Brief/Context

A client has approached me to make a wellness app that focuses on areas such as sleep, stress and having tools to help with daily things, to help with relaxing etc.

The Concept

I conducted research on mental health apps and created user flows, A/B tests, competitor analysis, and usability testing. My goal was to design an app that feels welcoming and inviting while incorporating features, like a skill tree, that encourage users to return regularly. I aimed to provide accessible and personalized support for individuals dealing with stress, anxiety, and emotional challenges. With a user-friendly interface and calming aesthetic, the app is designed to empower users to take control of their mental health and develop sustainable habits for well-being.

Deliverables

App Design, User Interface, UserExperience, Researching, Figma Prototype, Paper Prototyping
