Brief/Context

Eros Perfume is looking for an art nouveau inspired valentines perfume packaging that feels vintage and timeless for their upcoming release.

The Concept

I explored the Art Nouveau style and was inspired by its emphasis on fluid, organic lines and intricate detailing. I decided to incorporate these elements into the design, focusing on the flowing outer edges to evoke a sense of movement and elegance. Additionally, I wanted to bring in a touch of warmth and romance, so I included subtle Valentine's Day-inspired details, such as a heart motif on the flowing outer design. To further enhance the aesthetic, I added a 'harp' design to the outer elements, creating a harmonious and delicate balance between the flowing lines and the thoughtful symbolic touches.
